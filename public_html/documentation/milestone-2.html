User interaction:

The user will be able to select in the navbar the type of art they are interested in and the link will direct them to the part of the page containing that type of art. The bottom on the page will have a contact the artist section containing and email address.

Header:

The first thing you will see on the page will be a navbar that will link you to different parts of the page that contain different types of art. Each link in the navbar will specify what type of art it will send you to.

Meet the Artist:

The meet the artist section will have a brief introduction about the artist and have a headshot along with it.

Contact me:

This section will have a text box that says to contact the artist for custom art projects. It will also include the artists contact information.
