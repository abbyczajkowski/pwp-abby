Meet The Artist

Hi, my name is Grace Klein-Robbenhaar! I am finishing up my senior year of college at Fort Lewis in Durango. I study psychology, political science, and studio art! I am working to get my art out there and primarily work with oil paints and pen & ink, but also enjoy printmaking and multimedia. I hope you enjoy!
